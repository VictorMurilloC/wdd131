Project Site Plan

Site Name

Adventure Rafting Co.

This name represents an exciting and adventurous rafting company offering thrilling experiences for outdoor enthusiasts.

Site Purpose

The site serves as an informational hub for booking rafting trips, learning about safety measures, and exploring different river adventures.

Scenarios

  • How can I book a rafting trip?
  • What safety measures should I follow before going on a rafting adventure?

Color Schema

  • Dark Green (#3e5c57) - Used for headers and accents.
  • Light Teal (#53b5a0) - Used for background elements and highlights.

Typography

  • Georgia - Used for headings.
  • Roboto - Used for body text.
